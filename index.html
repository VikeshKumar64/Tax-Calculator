Tax Calculator
Tax Calculation Formula:

The tax calculation is based on the following formula:

  • Income under 8 Lakhs is not taxed.
  • Income over 8 Lakhs is taxed at different rates based on age:
    • 30% for age < 40
    • 40% for age ≥ 40 but < 60
    • 10% for age ≥ 60

For example, if someone aged 34 has an income of 40 Lakhs with no deductions, the tax would be 30% of the amount over 8 Lakhs.

  • <40
  • ≥ 40 & < 60
  • ≥ 60
